# AI - Recommendation

## Purpose
Recommendations help clients discover relevant professionals and help tradespeople discover suitable opportunities.

## Recommendation Types
- home-page suggestions for clients
- matching suggestions for tradespeople
- related services and upsell opportunities
- follow-up prompts for incomplete journeys

## Input Signals
- user intent
- service history
- location relevance
- availability and responsiveness
- trust and quality metrics

## Feedback Loop
The system should gather user feedback such as clicks, saves, contacts, bookings, dismissals, and complaints to refine ranking performance over time.

## Design Principles
- explainable outputs
- no hidden ranking manipulation
- clear separation between AI suggestion and final business decision
