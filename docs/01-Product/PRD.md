# Product Requirements Document

## Product Overview
Unamora is an AI-powered marketplace that connects clients with verified skilled tradespeople for urgent repairs, planned projects, and recurring services. The platform combines intelligent discovery, secure booking workflows, transparent communication, and verification-driven trust signals.

## Problem Statement
Clients often struggle to find dependable service providers quickly, while skilled professionals need a reliable path to reach qualified leads and manage their work professionally. Unamora addresses this by providing:
- trusted professional discovery
- clear booking and scheduling workflows
- secure messaging and payment coordination
- verification and review mechanisms
- operational tooling for administrators

## Core Goals
1. Help clients find suitable professionals quickly and confidently.
2. Help tradespeople receive relevant leads and manage bookings efficiently.
3. Give administrators visibility into marketplace health, risk, and compliance.
4. Create a scalable platform that supports AI-assisted discovery without compromising trust and safety.

## Primary User Groups
- Clients: individuals or businesses seeking skilled help.
- Tradespeople: verified professionals offering services.
- Admins: operations, support, compliance, and moderation staff.

## Key Features
- Search by service, location, availability, price, and verification status.
- AI-assisted matching and recommendations.
- Secure booking lifecycle with status tracking.
- Real-time messaging between client and professional.
- Verification workflows for identity, credentials, and documents.
- Review and reputation system.
- Admin dashboards for disputes, fraud, verification, and support cases.

## Functional Scope
### MVP
- user registration and authentication
- profile management
- search and filtering
- booking requests and confirmations
- basic review system
- admin moderation dashboard

### Future Enhancements
- dynamic pricing and promotions
- richer AI recommendations
- advanced fraud detection
- multi-language support
- marketplace analytics and reporting

## Success Metrics
- search-to-booking conversion rate
- average time to first booking
- verification completion rate
- customer satisfaction and review quality
- admin case resolution time

## Constraints and Risks
- trust and safety must remain central
- AI recommendations must be explainable and auditable
- compliance requirements must be respected for identity, payments, and document handling
