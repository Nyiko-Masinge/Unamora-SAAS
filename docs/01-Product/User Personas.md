# User Personas

## 1. Client
### Profile
A homeowner, business owner, or tenant who needs a service provider quickly and wants confidence in the quality and reliability of the provider.

### Goals
- find a suitable professional fast
- compare options based on location, availability, and reviews
- book and communicate securely
- track progress and payments clearly

### Pain Points
- unreliable or unverified providers
- poor communication
- difficulty comparing quotes or availability

## 2. Tradesperson
### Profile
A verified professional offering services such as plumbing, electrical work, construction support, cleaning, maintenance, or related trades.

### Goals
- gain visibility to relevant opportunities
- manage bookings and availability efficiently
- showcase reputation and credentials
- reduce admin overhead and no-shows

### Pain Points
- low-quality leads
- fragmented tools for booking and follow-up
- difficulty proving trustworthiness to prospective clients

## 3. Administrator
### Profile
A platform operator handling support, verification, disputes, compliance, and growth initiatives.

### Goals
- maintain marketplace quality and safety
- investigate fraud or misconduct
- monitor operational health and workflow queues
- support both clients and professionals effectively

### Pain Points
- scattered signals across fragmented systems
- delayed visibility into disputes or verification issues
- high operational cost without clear tooling

## 4. Support and Compliance Staff
### Profile
Staff members who review documents, approve profiles, resolve escalations, and monitor platform policy adherence.

### Needs
- strong audit trails
- clear review queues
- AI-assisted insights with human approval
